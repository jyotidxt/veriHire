# 🛡️ VeriHire — AI Career Intelligence Platform

> **An AI-powered Chrome Extension and Full Stack SaaS platform that helps job seekers analyze job opportunities, evaluate trust signals, optimize resumes, prepare for interviews, and manage their entire job search journey—all from one place.**

---

## 🚀 Overview

VeriHire is more than a job verification tool.

It is an **AI Career Intelligence Platform** designed to make job searching smarter, safer, and more efficient.

Using a Chrome Extension, VeriHire works directly inside job portals such as LinkedIn (with additional platform support planned). Instead of forcing users to copy and paste job descriptions into an AI chatbot, VeriHire automatically understands the job, analyzes it using AI, and presents structured insights within seconds.

The accompanying SaaS dashboard helps users manage applications, improve resumes, prepare for interviews, and monitor their job search with AI-powered recommendations.

## 🛡️ AI Job Trust Analysis

Instead of claiming whether a job is **"Fake"**, VeriHire provides explainable AI insights such as:

* Trust Score (0–100)
* Risk Level
* Company Verification Signals
* Suspicious Indicators
* AI Explanation
* Safety Recommendations

The platform is designed to help users make informed decisions—not absolute judgments.
